## Lighting Tips for Better Video
Even a 3000 SAR camera looks bad with poor lighting.
1.  **Face a window:** Natural light is free and flattering.
2.  **Avoid backlighting:** Don't sit with a bright window behind you.
3.  **Clean your lens:** A microfiber cloth can make a blurry image sharp instantly.
