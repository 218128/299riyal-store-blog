# Best Mechanical Keyboards Under 299 SAR in 2025

Once a luxury item, mechanical keyboards have become surprisingly affordable. You no longer need to spend 500+ SAR to get that satisfying click and tactile feedback.

Here are the best budget mechanical keyboards under 299 SAR.

## 1. Redragon K552 Kumara
*Approx. Price: 140 SAR*

The Redragon K552 is legendary in the budget gaming community. It's built like a tank and offers a true mechanical experience.

### Key Features:
*   **Switch Type:** Outemu Blue (Clicky)
*   **Size:** TKL (Tenkeyless - 87 keys)
*   **Backlight:** Red LED (RGB versions available)
*   **Build:** Metal and ABS construction

**Why we love it:** It's virtually indestructible. The keys are loud and clicky, perfect for typists who love feedback.

## 2. Royal Kludge RK61
*Approx. Price: 220 SAR*

The RK61 causes waves for being a wireless mechanical keyboard at a budget price. It's a 60% compact layout, perfect for saving desk space.

### Key Features:
*   **Connectivity:** Bluetooth / Wired / 2.4G
*   **Size:** 60% Compact
*   **Switches:** Hot-swappable (Red, Brown, or Blue)
*   **Lighting:** RGB

**Why we love it:** Hot-swappable switches at this price is a steal. You can upgrade the switches later without buying a new keyboard.

## 3. Logitech K835 TKL
*Approx. Price: 249 SAR*

For office workers who want a more "grown-up" look, the Logitech K835 is sleek, modern, and sturdy.

### Key Features:
*   **Switches:** TTC Red (Linear) or Blue (Clicky)
*   **Design:** Aluminum top case
*   **Layout:** TKL

**Why we love it:** It looks professional on any desk. Logitech reliability is a big plus.

## 4. E-Yooso Z11
*Approx. Price: 110 SAR*

One of the cheapest ways to get into mechanical keyboards. It's surprisingly decent for the price of a standard membrane keyboard.

### Key Features:
*   **Size:** 60% Mini
*   **Switches:** Hot-swappable
*   **Lighting:** Single color LED

**Why we love it:** Unbeatable price. Great for testing if you like mechanical keyboards before committing to a pricier one.

## Switch Guide for Beginners
*   **Blue Switches:** Loud click, tactile bump. Great for typing alone.
*   **Red Switches:** Smooth, linear, quiet. Best for gaming.
*   **Brown Switches:** Tactile bump, quiet. Good middle ground.

## Conclusion

If you want wireless freedom, the **Royal Kludge RK61** is the clear winner. For pure durability and value, the **Redragon K552** remains the king of budget boards.
