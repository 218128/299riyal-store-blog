# Top 5 Bluetooth Speakers Under 299 SAR for 2025

Great audio doesn't have to cost thousands of Riyals. Whether you want a speaker for your room, a small gathering, or outdoor adventures, there are some amazing budget options available in the Saudi market.

## 1. JBL Go 3
*Approx. Price: 169 SAR*

The JBL Go 3 is a legend in the ultra-portable category. It features a bold style and surprisingly rich JBL Pro Sound.

### Why buy it?
*   **IP67 Waterproof & Dustproof:** Take it to the pool or park without worry.
*   **Design:** Compact, rugged, and comes with an integrated loop.
*   **Battery:** Up to 5 hours of playtime.

**Verdict:** The best "clip-and-go" speaker you can buy.

## 2. Anker Soundcore 3
*Approx. Price: 249 SAR*

Anker's Soundcore line is famous for value. The Soundcore 3 upgrades the fan-favorite Soundcore 2 with better sound and titanium drivers.

### Why buy it?
*   **Sound:** 16W stereo sound with BassUp technology.
*   **Battery:** Massive 24-hour playtime.
*   **App Support:** Customize EQ via the Soundcore app.

**Verdict:** The best battery life in its class. Perfect for all-day listening.

## 3. Sony SRS-XB13
*Approx. Price: 199 SAR*

Sony's Extra Bass technology in a tiny can. The SRS-XB13 is compact but punches above its weight in the low-end frequencies.

### Why buy it?
*   **Sound:** Extra Bass for deep, punchy sound.
*   **Usability:** Multiway strap included.
*   **Durability:** IP67 rated.

**Verdict:** If you love bass but need portability, this is the one.

## 4. Tribit XSound Go
*Approx. Price: 140 SAR*

A lesser-known brand that audio enthusiasts swear by. The Tribit XSound Go offers balanced, clear sound that rivals much more expensive brands.

### Why buy it?
*   **Sound:** Crystal clear highs and mids.
*   **Waterproof:** IPX7 rating.
*   **Value:** Incredible sound-per-riyal value.

**Verdict:** The "budget audiophile" choice.

## Conclusion

For pure portability, grab the **JBL Go 3**. If you need room-filling sound and battery that lasts for days, the **Anker Soundcore 3** is the unbeatable champion under 299 SAR.
